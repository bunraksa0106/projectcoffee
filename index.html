Our Story

Founded in 2010 , coffee haven began with a simple mission: to create a warm, welcoming space where coffee lovers could enjoy exceptional brews in a cozy atmosphere.

We source our beans ethically from small farms around the world, roast them in-house and serve them with care .every cup is crafted with attention to detail and passion for the perfect brew.
